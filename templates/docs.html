Getting Started

Welcome to YouTube Video Summarizer! This guide will help you get up and running in minutes.

Step 1: Sign In

Click the "Sign in with Google" button to authenticate with your Google account. This is required to access all features.

Step 2: Paste a YouTube URL

Enter any YouTube video URL into the input field. The app supports standard YouTube URLs and short URLs (youtu.be).

Step 3: Get the Transcript

Click "Get Transcript" to extract the full transcript from the video. This uses advanced speech recognition AI.

Step 4: Generate Summaries

Click "Get Summaries" to generate platform-specific summaries optimized for Telegram, Discord, Twitter, and LinkedIn.

How to Use

Main Interface Overview

The main app interface has the following sections:

  • URL Input: Paste your YouTube video URL here
  • Video Details: Shows title, channel, duration, and view count
  • Transcript: Full text transcript of the video
  • Social Media Posts: Generated summaries for each platform

Copy & Share

Each summary has a "Copy Text" button that copies it to your clipboard. You can then paste it directly into your social media posts.

Direct Posting

Use the "Post to [Platform]" buttons to post directly to your social media accounts (requires authentication with those services).

Video Transcription

The transcription feature converts video audio into text using AI-powered speech recognition.

üí° How It Works

The app downloads the audio from the YouTube video and processes it through Whisper (OpenAI's speech recognition model) to generate an accurate transcript.

Processing Time

Transcription time depends on video length. A 10-minute video typically takes 30-60 seconds to process.

Accuracy

The Whisper model has ~94% accuracy for English audio. Accuracy may vary for non-English content or audio with background noise.

Supported Languages

English is primarily supported. The model can handle other languages but may have reduced accuracy.

Summarization

Summaries are generated using state-of-the-art AI models to extract the most important information from transcripts.

ü§ñ AI Model Used

Facebook's BART (Bidirectional and Auto-Regressive Transformers) model is used for high-quality summarization.

Platform-Specific Summaries

Each social media platform receives a summary optimized for its format:

  • Telegram: Medium-length, informative summaries
  • Discord: Concise, engaging summaries with emojis
  • Twitter: Short, punchy tweets (limited characters)
  • LinkedIn: Professional, detailed summaries

Edit Summaries

You can edit any generated summary directly in the text area before posting or copying.

Scheduling Posts

Schedule your social media posts to go live at a specific time, and let the system handle automatic posting.

How to Schedule

  2. Select a platform and choose "Schedule for Later"
  3. Pick the date and time you want the post to go live
  4. Confirm - the system will post automatically at the scheduled time

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What video sources are supported?

A: Currently, we support YouTube videos. The app can handle standard YouTube URLs and shortened youtu.be links.

Q: How long does transcription take?

A: Processing time varies based on video length. Most videos are transcribed within 30-120 seconds.

Q: Can I edit summaries?

A: Yes! All generated summaries can be edited directly in the text area before posting or copying.

Q: Is my data secure?

A: Yes, all data is encrypted and processed securely. We never share your data with third parties without permission.

Q: What happens to the videos I process?

A: Videos are downloaded temporarily for processing and deleted afterward. Transcripts and summaries are stored for your reference.

Q: Can I post to multiple platforms at once?

A: Yes! You can post to multiple platforms by clicking each platform's "Post" button, or schedule posts for later.

Q: What if transcription fails?

A: If a video can't be transcribed, you'll see an error message. This usually means the audio is too unclear or the video format isn't supported.
